By Loghan Hornor and Angel Jimenez

This is the first game I’ve made, I worked with Angel on it. I focused on the story and dialogue while Angel scripted the combat. I designed the website using Figma and flowcharts. The text adventure is about how a farmer’s life was derailed and how he goes after the man who destroyed his life.

About Vengeful Knight

This is a group project using JavaScript, CSS, and HTML. This was our first game we created from scratch, it is a text adventure. We spent the first week learning about other text adventures, then came up with the story. We presented the final product to our class as well as at the CART Showcase.
